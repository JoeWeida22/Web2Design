About Tony Stark

Tony Stark, the man behind the Iron Man armor, is a billionaire inventor and philanthropist. He created the first Iron Man suit in a cave to escape captivity and has since evolved into one of the most powerful and intelligent superheroes in the Marvel universe.

His journey includes founding the Avengers, battling threats like Thanos, and ultimately making the ultimate sacrifice to save the universe.
